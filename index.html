Welcome to

Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology (Dr AlT) was founded in the year 1980 by Panchajanya Vidyapeeta Welfare Trust (PVPWT) Bangalore, PVPWT is founded by Late Sri. M. H. Jayaprakash Narayan. On account of 42 years of it's establishment RUBY JUBILEE is being celebrated this year marking another milestone in the history of the institute with great pride.

Sanskruthi

We are back and bigger

Dr Ambedkar institute of technology celebrates it's annual inter collegiate techno cultural fest SANSKRUTHI which showcasing the most talented young minds of the college portraying professionalism through a plethora of techno cultural events. The fest attracts thousands of students from various colleges across State. This makes it one of the largest conglomeration of students in a single location in the State. The 26 acre campus of Dr. AlT is host to over 5000 students and is located on outer ring road, situated in a hub of educational institutes like Bangalore University, RVCE, ISSATE. SJBIT. PESIT.
